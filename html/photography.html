Fall is the most beautiful season of the year. That is when the leaves turn colors to orange and brown-ish. In addition, the weather is a little bit cold with wind breeze but the sun still shines bright. The combination creates a yellow organge tone for the screen as well as it brings the warm atmosphere to screen. These pictures are shown in this website are msotly taken by myself. As I enjoy the weather very much, I try to documentation every single moment of it in both Hanoi and Chicago.

Hanoi

Hanoi is very beautiful during the fall season. As the weather turns a little cold along with the sun that shining light, it makes the atmosphere very lightly with fresh air. The atmosphere also adds the colour tone to the scenery in which it makes the building looks more vintage in some ways. All of these factors: wind, sun, colours, and building, blend into each other establishing a peaceful and relaxing environment.

Chicago

Similar with Hanoi, Chicago is also very beautiful during the fall season. What I love most in Chicago, especially at IIT, is the rows of the tree that their leaves changes the colours. These trees create the pattern of red, orange, and yellow colours, and bring more lives and colourfulness to the simplicity and minimalist of IIT campus.
